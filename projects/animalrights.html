Animal Rights Website

Development March 2024

Design and development of the new website for the non-profit organization Animal Rights with WordPress.

Role:
Web Design · WordPress Development · Content Management · SEO Optimization

Project Overview

Design and development of the new website for the non-profit organization Animal Rights. The project focused on creating an impactful platform to raise awareness and drive action for animal welfare causes. The website needed to be both informative and emotionally compelling to inspire visitors to take action.

Key Features

  • Donation system integration for fundraising
  • Campaign management tools for organizing events
  • News and blog section for updates and stories
  • Volunteer registration system
  • Event calendar for protests and awareness campaigns
  • Resource library for educational materials
  • Contact forms for inquiries and support

Technologies Used

  • WordPress for content management
  • Custom plugin development for specialized functionality
  • Payment gateway integration for donations
  • Email marketing system integration
  • Social media integration for sharing
  • SEO optimization for better visibility

Design Challenges

Creating a website that effectively communicates the urgency of animal rights issues while maintaining a professional and trustworthy appearance. The challenge was to balance emotional impact with clear information architecture, ensuring visitors could easily find ways to help while being moved by the content to take action.

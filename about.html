My name is Daphne Varekamp and I'm a creative digital creator based in The Hague, Netherlands. In 2025, I started this freelance business to combine web design, video editing, and digital creativity into one versatile service.

DaphDesign is all about bringing ideas to life, whether it's designing and developing websites or crafting compelling visuals through video editing. I'm passionate about clean, intuitive design and always stay ahead of the curve by integrating the latest (AI) trends into my work.

DaphDesign is here to help bring your vision to reality. Let's create something great together!
